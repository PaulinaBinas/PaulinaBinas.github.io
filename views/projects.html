VR Interior Visualization

Description: Application prepared for my diploma thesis, that was inspired by the problems my father (stained-glass artist) faces in his job, when designing windows for clients. The software allows its user to enter a virtual interior and visualize a room by choosing its type, the furniture and the stained-glass window. Once inside the visualized room, one is also given the possibility to manipulate interior and exterior lighting, to observe how its changes might affect the colored-shadows cast by the window.
State: Finished
Technologies used: Unreal Engine 4, 3Ds Max, HTC Vive HMD
Project presentation video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4foPzTHTmto
